Dr. Vaijayanti Bhoraskar

Dr. Vaijayanti Bhoraskar is an Obstetrician and Gynaecologist. She obtained her medical education from B.J. Medical College, Pune (BJMC). After a stint at the Ruby Hall Clinic, Pune , she finished her residency at Nowrosjee Wadia Hospital, Bombay.
